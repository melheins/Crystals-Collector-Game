Crystal Collector

To win the game you need to collect the correct crystals to match the computers score.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Match your score to the computers number to win. Go above or below the computers score and you lose. Each time the game starts the computer score and the value of each crystal will change.
